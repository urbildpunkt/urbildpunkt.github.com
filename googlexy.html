Items Dashboard

A catalogue of everything you own or share, easily keep track of your items.

Search

Google Dashboard helps you find your things faster, any item is instantly loacted through search. It evens gives you a quick snapshot of what you are looking for, allowing you to see all the information you need right from search.

Info

Quickly find detailed information on any product, even at the supermarket. Google Dashboard shows the most relevant information at a glance, but can easily audit any items complete metahistory.

Alerts

Say goodbye to worrying about hidden ingredients, Google Dashboard automatically alerts you any time a product may conflict with your diet. It also notifies you when prodcuts are expired or recalled, keeping you safer.

Create & Collaborate

Find more than just information, a community surrounds every object that is ready to answer questions or engage in discussion.

Google Dashboard lets you do more than just create an object, it also allows for a community to form around it that leverages information, experience, and expertise. Every object becomes a process of self-realization, shaped by the interactions of the users themselves in collaboration with one another.

Places Dashboard

A directory of all the places you have access, easily manage who you give privileges to.

Manage

Google Dashboard lets you manage all of your places, from your home to your office. It even keeps track of your shared spaces, from the public library to your members-only gym, all in one place.

Status

Quickly check the status of any one of your places, including any of the items associated with it. You never have to wonder if you left the front door unlocked or forgot to return that library book.

Alerts

Say goodbye to worrying about security, Google Dashboard automatically alerts you of any suspicious activity within one of your places. It also allows you to quickly notify the proper authorities when necessary.

Share

Google Dashboard allows you to easily grant your Google+ circles special privileges to your own places, making sure you only share what you want with the right people. Access to spaces is also automatically shared with you based on the circles to which you belong, such as those of your work, school, or group memberships.

Utilities Dashboard

A register of all the services you consume, easily examine your costs and usage.

Monitor

Google Dashboard lets you monitor all of your utilities, from energy usage to even waste production. It not only allows you to keep track of your costs, but also your environmental imapact as well.

Ubiquity

Quickly gain access to your services anywhere you go. You never have to wonder about internet or electrical access, Google Dashboard automatically documents your usage and bills you accordingly.

Alerts

Say goodbye to worrying about hidden charges, with Google Dashboard you can always see how much you are using and its costs. It will even alert you of any changes that you may be unaware of.
