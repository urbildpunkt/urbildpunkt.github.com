Urbildpunkt.

Urbildpunkt.

(German)
Urbild: prototype, archetype + punkt: pixel, point.


Hello there.

Urbildpunkt., a combination of two German words that is perhaps no more easier to spell than it is to try and pronounce, but I have chosen it all the same. In a time when every possible domain and username is already taken, I wanted to find something that was unique enough to always be available. But it is more to me than that, I wanted to choose something with meaning. Loosely translating the name a friend and I once used, protopixel, I came up with something that isn't just a word, but a sentence, a description, a process. And that is why I have decided to end it with a period. The meaning can be interpreted in many ways, from the literal prototype or archetype of a design to the actual process of prototyping itself, all of which seem relevant to me. So if it is an unusual choice or a perfect fit, I guess I will just have to give it my all and see.
